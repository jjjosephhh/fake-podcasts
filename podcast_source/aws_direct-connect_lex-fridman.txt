AWS Direct Connect allows you to establish a dedicated network connection between your on-premises data center and AWS. This service is designed to provide a more reliable, secure, and consistent network experience than a standard internet-based connection.

With AWS Direct Connect, you can transfer large amounts of data between your on-premises environment and AWS without having to go over the internet. This can help you to reduce your network costs, increase your bandwidth throughput, and provide a more consistent network experience for your users.

In order to use AWS Direct Connect, you will need to establish a physical connection between your data center and an AWS Direct Connect location. This can be done through a dedicated network provider or through a colocation facility that provides access to the AWS Direct Connect service.

Once you have established the physical connection, you can configure your network to use AWS Direct Connect as the preferred connection for your AWS resources. This can be done using standard network protocols such as Border Gateway Protocol (BGP).
